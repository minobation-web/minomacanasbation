HOWDY! I am LUX

(Princess of demacia)

Lux hails from the kingdom of Demacia, an insular realm where magic is viewed with fear and suspicion. She is from a prestigious, anti-magic noble family (the Crownguards) and is the younger sister of the champion Garen. Lux possesses the innate ability to control and manipulate light magic, a power she was forced to keep secret, which is a core conflict in her story.

ABOUT US
